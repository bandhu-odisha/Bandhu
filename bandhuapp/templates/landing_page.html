    About Us

    Bandhu is an idea that celebrates goodness, that is in you, me and all others.

    It is a group of people, good but not necessarily great, bound by a spontaneous flow of boudless friendship. It is concerned towards the inconsistencies within and without. Bandhu does small things with highest possible sincerity like running weekly sessions of yoga, game, song and story telling. It leads the movement against liquor both locally and at state level. It's brand programme Ankurayan is in its 13th year and a houshold name in Tirtol, Garadpur and some part of cuttack dist. It strives to revive the village spirit and check, rather reverse the mindless migration to cities.
